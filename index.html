Story

Our Love Story

First Meet

We first crossed paths at the Health Education Department of the University of Ilorin. She was engaged in personal evangelism but found it a bit challenging to approach me with her message. Eventually, she mustered the courage to share the Gospel with me, and that marked the beginning of our 'Forever' journey.

First Date

She was taking a trip to Ilorin and I told her to have a stop over at Ibadan so we could meet and greet. She agreed, we went to chicken republic, Bodija and it was a sweet one. I saw her off to the park as per perfect gentleman. Lol

Proposal

On the 8th of January 2021, he popped the biggest question "Will you marry me" in an amusement park in the heart of Osogbo, Osun State. She was unsuspecting! In that magical moment, we both decided to take a walk... As we stood up, everyone came around us as pre-planned I stood to make my love confessions to her and I felt her nervousness I got down on one knee, and brought out a small velvet box, revealing a glistening ring that reflected the moonlight My words, filled with love and vulnerability, echoed through the park as I asked the question that would forever change our lives
